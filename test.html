Larry uses the fitness calendar app to solve his routine difficulties. Larry establishes his daily workouts on the calendar so he will not forget, each detailing what he should be doing. Furthermore, Larry has adopted a better diet to help in his weight loss process and has developed a schedule using the app of what he is eating each day, monitoring his calorie intake as well.
